El Capitan is the pinnacle of most climbers' achievments

The Dawn Wall was considered impossible to free climb by most because it is a sheer face with limited features. Tommy Caldwell and Kevin Jorgeson dedicated six years of their life making it possible. There has only been one other climber crazy enough to ascend the Dawn Wall, and that is the world renowned, alien of a human, Adam Ondra. Ondra had never climbed many big walls prior to attempting the hardest big wall route in the world, at the most prestigious location of big wall climbing. He heard about the ascent and after getting a minimal amount of practice in beforehand, he decided to give it a try. It took Adam 8 days to complete the route.

Revolution

It all started with a horde of hippies who essentialy squatted in Yosemite national park, calling it their home for an extended period of time. They were later referred to as the Stonemasters. Ever since the Stonemasters, the sport of climbing has progressed at a breakneck pace. There are new grades, or difficulties, being discovered every year. What's most impressive to me is how young some of the strongest climbers in the world are. The reason for this is that in order to be a consistently strong climber, you need a high strength index. This is the essentially measuring how strong you are according to your body weight.

Passion

Climbing is one of my passions. I discovered it while hikihg through Yosemite with a friend. He decided to make a pit stop on our way home to check out some rocks in Tuolumne Meadows and I immediately fell in love. It combines everything I love into one sport; strength, endurance, precision and technique. All of this and you get to see some of the most beautiful places while doing it. Each style of climbing requires a unique blend of skill, strength, and endurance. Bouldering, speed and sport climbing were recently added to the olympics.
